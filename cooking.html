Peanut Butter Oats

April 01, 2023

A healthy snack I'm liking at the minute.



Less than 300 calories and takes 1 minute to prepare:

- 50g of dry rolled oats

- 100mL of milk

- 30g of peanut butter

- (optional) zero sugar flavoring

- Zap in the microwave for 45 seconds.

- Mix together.


Enjoy.
